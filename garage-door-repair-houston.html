What We Fix Every Day

If your garage door is doing any of this, we can be on site today.

⚠

Broken Spring

Loud snap, door won't lift, or one side sagging. The most common repair we do.

⚙

Opener Issue

Remote not working, motor running but door not moving, or no power at all.

↯

Off-Track Door

Door hanging crooked or jammed in the rails. Don't force it — we'll reset it safely.

✕

Won't Open or Close

Stuck halfway, reverses on its own, or completely unresponsive. We diagnose on site.

♪

Noisy or Jerky

Grinding, squealing, or jumpy travel usually means rollers, hinges, or spring tension.

▢

Damaged Panels

Backed into the door? We replace single panels or full sections without a full tear-out.
