Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about garage door repair, installation, springs, openers, and maintenance in Houston.

Are you a locally owned business?
Yes. Element Doors & Garages is a locally owned and operated business serving Houston and the surrounding communities since 2003.
What types of doors do you work on?
Residential and commercial garage doors, overhead doors, entry doors, and custom door solutions. Steel, wood, glass, and aluminum.
How long does a garage door installation take?
Most standard installations are done in about 3–5 hours. Custom or complex installs can take longer — we'll give you a realistic window with the quote.
Do you offer same-day garage door repairs?
Yes. Garage door issues can't wait, so we offer same-day service for most repairs across the Houston area.
Do you provide 24/7 emergency service?
Yes. We provide 24/7 emergency garage door repair and spring service throughout the Houston area. Most after-hours calls reach a real person, often arriving on site within an hour or two.
What areas do you serve?
Houston, Katy, Sugar Land, Cypress, The Woodlands, Pearland, Pasadena, Spring, League City, Tomball, Kingwood, Conroe, Baytown, and surrounding areas.
How often should I have my garage door serviced?
At least once a year. Regular maintenance — lubrication, tension check, hardware tightening, sensor alignment — costs less than the spring replacement it prevents.
What do I do if my spring breaks?
Stop using the door immediately. A broken spring makes the door dangerously heavy and the opener can be damaged trying to lift it. Call us right away.
Why won't my garage door open or close?
Common causes include a broken spring, misaligned safety sensors, dead remote batteries, a worn opener, or a snapped cable. We diagnose on site and explain what's actually wrong.
Should I repair or replace my garage door?
If your door is over 15 years old, heavily damaged, or breaking down often, replacement is usually the more cost-effective option. We'll give you both prices and let you decide.
How do I keep bugs and leaves out of my garage?
Weatherstripping, perimeter seals, and threshold seals do most of the work. We can install all three.
Why choose a professional over the cheapest option?
Garage door springs and overhead doors are heavy machinery. Bad work leads to safety hazards, more breakdowns, and higher costs in the long run. We charge a fair price to do it once, correctly.
Do you offer maintenance packages?
Yes. An annual package saves money, extends the life of the door, and reduces emergency breakdowns. Ask us for current pricing.
Do you work on residential and commercial doors?
Both. Residential repairs and installs, commercial repairs, commercial rolling steel doors, spring service, and garage door motors.
