About

Houston's Garage Door Company Since 2003

Family-owned, locally operated, and run by the same crew that does the work. Built on showing up, doing the job right, and answering the phone.

Element Doors & Garages started in Houston in 2003. Two decades later, we're still a family-owned business — same trucks, same neighborhoods, same people on the phone.

We service residential and commercial garage doors across Houston and the surrounding communities. Steel, wood, glass, modern aluminum — we install, repair, and maintain them all. Our work also includes overhead doors, entry doors, and custom door solutions.

What we do

  • 24/7 emergency garage door repair and spring service
  • Residential and commercial repairs
  • New garage door installations
  • Spring replacement
  • Annual maintenance packages
  • Commercial rolling steel doors

Why we work this way

There are cheaper garage door companies in Houston. We're not pretending otherwise. What we sell is the version where the tech actually shows up, the spring is the right gauge, the price doesn't change halfway through, and you don't have to call back in three months because something else broke.

Bad garage door work isn't just annoying — torsion springs and overhead doors are heavy machinery. A wrong-sized spring or a half-installed cable can hurt someone. We'd rather quote a fair price than cut a corner that costs more later.
